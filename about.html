We Are The Teens With Diffence.

At christ gospel international (Karu Church) there has always been a proposal for a teens church, and as at last year through the driving force of the teenagers co-ordinator in person of Sis Kehinde Abiola we were able to get the teens church.First service which was held on the second sunday of november 2022 was very entertaining and we all learnt alot.

Our Purpose.

In general our purpose is to capture young teenagers who are yet to discover themselves and bring them to the understanding of the word of God.

Our Mission.

Recuiting new Kingdom members for the expansion of the kingdom of God.

I can do all things through him who strengthens me.

What We Seek To Achieve

Identity in Christ.
Becoming Kingdom Members.
Becoming Bible Scholars.
Strong Reletionship with christ.
